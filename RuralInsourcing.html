Rural Insourcing

If your organization is considering an “outsource” model or you simply are looking for a cost effective Managed Services Provider, call AGILE GLOBAL to discuss how Rural Insourcing could benefit your corporate goals.

In this global economy, some organizations have adopted an offshore model to capitalize on an aggressive rate structure.

While few have seen this model provide extensive cost savings and value, many have been frustrated by the challenges of this approach and realize in order to justify the move, they must contend with the following:

  • Increased development lifecycles due to different business understanding/li>
  • Decreased critical thought which delays or jeopardizes project timelines
  • High attrition rates
  • Lack of accessibility to the entire development team
  • Time zone barriers
  • Social and cultural barriers
  • Extensive travel expense and time

These issues can be overcome with time and significant investment, however, a general rule for offshore is, “Go big, go long or don’t go at all!” AGILE GLOBAL provides a unique alternative which allows you significant cost savings, comparable to offshore, while overcoming the challenges mentioned above.

AGILE GLOBAL’s Rural Insource Center is in a strategic location within the United States which allows access to all the services of a large metro area, yet provides significant cost savings. Combining our location selection with our unique model of acquiring and managing our highly skilled resources, we are able to provide:

  • Streamlined SDLC
  • Cohesive social, cultural, and business understanding
  • Critical feedback during the entire project
  • Full team accessibility for our clients
  • Low attrition rates
  • Up to 50% saving over tradition models
  • Decreased concern for highly regulated/secure data
  • Easy and cost effective access to our facility
